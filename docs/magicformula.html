🪄 Magic Formula Ranks
Method (Joel Greenblatt): every company is ranked by ROCE (higher = better) and by EV/EBITDA (lower = better); Total Rank = ROCE rank + EV/EBITDA rank — lowest wins. Ranks recompute within your current filter, so filtering one sector reproduces a sector-wise magic-formula list. Sector badge = the sector engine's head/tailwind signal. Financials are flagged because EV/EBITDA is not meaningful for lenders. Rules-based screen — do your own due diligence; NOT investment advice.
